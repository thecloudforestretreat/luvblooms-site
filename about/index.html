About LuvBlooms

Beautiful moments, simplified.

Luvblooms was born from a family passion for beautiful moments and the belief that everyone can create them at home. We simplify floral styling through curated DIY recipes that make arranging flowers easy, inspiring, and accessible.

What we do

Every stem is thoughtfully selected so you can design your own arrangements with confidence, no florist experience needed. Whether it is a weekly ritual, a gift, or a moment of calm in your space, luvblooms helps you create something uniquely yours.

Mission

Luvblooms simplifies beautiful living through curated DIY floral recipes that inspire creativity and elevate everyday spaces. We believe in fresh blooms, simple rituals, and empowering everyone to create something uniquely their own.

What we stand for

At Luvblooms, we believe that beauty should feel effortless. We stand for making floral design simple, inspiring, and accessible to everyone, not just florists or creatives. Through curated DIY floral recipes and fresh, premium stems, we empower you to create something uniquely your own.

Creativity Made Simple

We take the guesswork out of arranging. Our curated recipes, thoughtful guidance, and step-by-step tips help you feel confident, inspired, and excited to create.

Freshness and Quality First

Every stem is intentionally selected for its beauty, longevity, and seasonality. Backed by Flowermarketplace, we ensure unmatched sourcing and freshness from farm to doorstep.

Intentional Living

We believe in the power of simple rituals like arranging flowers to elevate everyday moments. A moment of calm, a spark of joy, a way to make your space feel more you.

Accessible Beauty

Beautiful arrangements should not require a florist or a special skill. We make the art of flowers approachable, enjoyable, and proudly DIY.

Thoughtful Design

Our aesthetic is minimal, modern, and elevated, never tacky. We curate with intention so what arrives at your home feels refined, inspiring, and timeless.

Warm and Romantic

Luvblooms is rooted in love, gifting, and heartfelt moments. We bring a warm, approachable luxury feel to modern flower rituals.
